Favourite Hobby

Reading

I’m a firm believer that life is better lived between the pages of a book. While I’ve explored countless worlds, I always find myself drawn back to the queen of crime, Agatha Christie. I thought I knew how mysteries worked until I read And Then There Were None.

The book didn't just tell a story; it completely dismantled my logic and left me speechless by the final chapter, when the actual culprit is revealed.
